Project Management Analyst

Job Description:

Provide project management support in the initiating, planning, executing, monitoring & controlling, and closing of projects.

Ensure the accuracy of project cost, schedule, and performance data to support project and organizational decision making. Monitor commitment, obligation, and expenditure data from Navy ERP and internal financial tracking tools and trackers, identify project risks, and develop mitigation strategies. Develop sponsor Monthly Status Reports (MSRs) summarizing cost, schedule, and technical health of projects. Maintain financial spreadsheets, databases, and dashboards and monitor project authorized amounts, burn rates, and balances. Develop and run recurring and ad hoc financial reports, review financial data on labor, travel, material, and service contract transactions, and coordinate with internal project teams to execute project funds.

Qualifications:
  • Active Secret Clearance
  • Bachelor's degree
  • At least 3 years of experience in DoD project management or
    financial management
  • Proficiency in MS Office tools
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Experience in Navy ERP is preferred

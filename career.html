Employee Testimonals

What is the best thing about working at Nine 30?

The best thing (besides the company tab at St. Inie's) is that you are always recognized for your work, whether it's supporting your client, collaborating with another contractor, or assisting a coworker! Nine 30 is so diverse and welcoming and has an amazing team culture. With so many different skill sets and ideas, it’s easy to learn something new or look at a problem from a different angle.

- Kali

What makes Nine 30 different?

The people! The team has really invested in a positive, supportive culture here at Nine 30 and that shines. It sets Nine 30 apart from other contractors. Also, I’ve been surprised by the telework flexibility! Having a family and a spouse who is frequently deployed can make in-office hours tough certain days. Having the ability to telework from a home office helps me continue to support my projects and clients, while also being there for my family.

- Kate

How would you describe your job satisfaction at Nine 30?

My overall job satisfaction is off the charts. The work-life balance is exceptional with great benefits and a flexible work schedule. My position also fits exactly with my skills, ability, and career goals. Best of all, the people at Nine 30 are truly exceptional and care about continuing to cultivate the work culture we have worked diligently to create.

- Lauren

What does your current job have that your previous jobs have not?

My current job has so much flexibility with work. I can work from home when my family is sick or other various complications arise. Nine 30 leadership is extremely understanding regarding work life balance.

- JC

What has surprised you most about working at Nine 30?

I was really surprised by how great the benefits are, being a small company. Coming from a larger company with less than desirable health insurance coverage, I'm immensely pleased with the plans offered here, especially the zero deductible option and how much Nine 30 contributes to the premiums.

- Sam

How has Nine 30 helped you develop and grow your skills?

The team is so kind, patient, and encouraging in helping me learn new processes and develop my skills. There is a lot of hands-on training and a support system in place, but I love that I am empowered to support my clients on my own. I can make the decisions and can find solutions that work for me and the client without going through any red tape. Also, my current job has an incredible amount of flexibility, it has made my life as a single mother much easier to navigate.

- Rebekah

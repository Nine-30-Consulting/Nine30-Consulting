Junior Project Management Analyst

Job Description:

Provide project management support in the initiating, planning, executing, monitoring & controlling, and closing of projects.

Ensure the accuracy of project cost, schedule, and performance data to support project and organizational decision making. Develop internal and external project status reports, identify project risks, and develop mitigation strategies. Maintain financial spreadsheets and monitor project authorized amounts, burn rates, and balances. Develop funds execution strategies, monitor financial data on labor, travel, material, and service contract transactions, and coordinate with Project Leads and internal project teams to execute project tasking.

Qualifications:
  • Active Secret Clearance
  • Bachelor's degree
  • At least 1 year of experience in project management or
    financial management
  • Proficiency in MS Office tools
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Experience in Navy ERP is preferred

Data Scientist

Job Location:

Hybrid with primarily Work From Home (WFH), but with occasional meetings at the Nine 30 office in Lexington Park, MD.

Job Description:

Provide data analytics support to the Naval Air Warfare Center Aircraft Division (NAWCAD) Comptroller and Business & Financial Management Community (BFMC) to provide real-time data to improve organizational decision making.

We are looking for a Qlik Developer, Data Analyst, or Data Scientist with at least 3 years of experience in business intelligence and at least one year of experience with Qlik to join our Business Intelligence team. The successful candidate will be responsible for creating, implementing, and maintaining Qlik dashboards, reports, and data models. Work closely with Comptroller, BFMs, project managers, financial analysts, and acquisition specialists to understand their needs and requirements and apply these to the build/test phase of reports and dashboards. Identify opportunities to improve data visualization tools and streamline reporting to make processes more automated and less manual. Develop and manage business intelligence solutions for the organization.

Qualifications:
  • Active Secret Clearance
  • Bachelor's degree
  • At least 3 years of experience in business intelligence
  • Proficiency in Qlik
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Knowledge of DoD acquisition, financial, and DoD PPBE processes preferred

Capabilities

Nine 30 strives to deliver premiere analytical services to the federal government
in the disciplines of financial management, project management, data analytics, and
acquisition while offering unparalleled career opportunities, experiences,
and benefits to our employees.

FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT

Deep subject matter expertise in financial management developing cost estimates, burn rate analysis, and financial reporting for projects, tracking new orders, expenditures, and carryover for organizational financial health assessments, supporting NAWCAD Commander initiative to improve funds acceptance, and developing dashboards to measure Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for Comptroller and Business Financial Manager (BFM) community.

Learn More

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Experienced Project Management Professionals (PMPs) providing full life cycle project management in the areas of initiating, planning, executing, monitoring & controlling, and closing for projects ranging from $10K to $75M.

Learn More

DATA ANALYTICS

Aggregating, evaluating, and cleansing complex financial, acquisition, and technical data and developing real-time, automated data visualizations through various tools to enable data-driven decision making.

Learn More

ACQUISITION

Extensive experience developing and executing acquisition strategies for service and hardware requirements, working directly with CORs to plan and monitor contract ceiling, funded levels, and burn rates, and developing hardware procurement package and tracking material.

JSE Acquisition Analyst

Job Location:

Hybrid with 3 days a week at the government facility at Pax River and 2 days a week split between the Nine 30 office in Lexington Park and Work From Home (WFH).

Job Description:

Provide acquisition support to the Joint Simulation Environment (JSE) Division at Patuxent River supporting service contract and hardware contract procurement actions.

Provide service acquisition support and develop acquisition strategies and plans by considering current and future project needs. Develop Procurement Initiation Documents (PIDs) and track Procurement Requests (PRs) through Navy ERP. Support CORs/ACORs and Acquisition Cell with post award contract management across a portfolio of contracts. Support the Project Leads on managing, monitoring, and reporting on specific projects Task Orders (TOs). Determine long term acquisition and resource requirements.

Provide hardware acquisition support and monitor procurements to ensure project schedules are met. Assist in translating technical requirements into acquisition documentation, conduct market research, and develop Bill of Materials (BOM) and Material Equipment Lists (MELs). Draft procurement documentation, track material from procurement planning to delivery, and provide project material tracking reports to Acquisition Cell and Project Lead stakeholders.

Qualifications:
  • Active Secret Clearance
  • Bachelor's degree
  • At least 3 years of experience in DoD acquisition management or
    procurement management
  • Proficiency in MS Office Tools
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Experience in Navy ERP and Procurement Management Tool (PMT)

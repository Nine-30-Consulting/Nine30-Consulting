Our History

Since our founding in 2019, we have expanded our portfolio
to 6 contracts and doubled as a team every year. We just
hired the 20th member of our team!

JAN.2019

Launch of
Nine 30 Consulting

FEB.2019

Begin supporting the NAWCAD Comptroller and the Funds Acceptance SharePoint Tool (FAST) project under the ManTech NCR contract

AUG.2019

Begin supporting the Special Communication Mission Solutions (SCMS) Division under the Booz Allen Hamilton SCP contract

SEP.2019

Join the MIL SATCOM contract to support the SCMS Division

JUN.2020

Team grows to 5 to meet increased FAST and SCMS requirements

DEC.2020

Complete NAWCAD command-wide deployment of FAST with 39 deployments to 216 unique organizations in just under 2 years
